# tunk

- **Grammatical Classification**: Noun
- **Etymology:** Unknown path, possibly from PrAtr [*quetil*](lexicon/lex/q/quetil.md) or [*quente*](lexicon/lex/q/quente.md)

1. tongue
2. dialect

## Forms
*tunkin*: (plural) tongues, languages
